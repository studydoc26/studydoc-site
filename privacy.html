Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

StudyDoc provides educational exam-preparation material for medical students and doctors. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use studydoc.co.in.

Information you provide

If you report a question or correction, we may collect the email address you enter, the correction details, the question/page being reported, and technical information needed to process the report. Reports are processed through StudyDoc's Cloudflare Worker and forwarded to Formspree.

Information stored on your device

Some question banks use browser local storage to remember quiz progress, answered questions, and reset state. This is stored in your browser and is used to make the practice bank easier to continue later.

Technical and service data

StudyDoc is delivered through Cloudflare. Cloudflare may process technical request data such as IP address, browser details, request URLs, security signals, and logs needed to deliver, protect, and measure the site. We may also use basic Cloudflare analytics or similar site-performance/security tools.

Third-party services

StudyDoc links to or uses third-party services including Formspree, YouTube, Instagram, Telegram, GitHub and Cloudflare. These services may process data under their own privacy policies. External PDFs and videos may also be hosted or loaded through third-party infrastructure.

Cookies

StudyDoc does not intentionally use advertising cookies. Cloudflare or embedded/linked third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies for security, media playback, analytics, or account features.

How we use information

  • To respond to correction reports or contact requests.
  • To improve question quality, explanations, resources and site reliability.
  • To protect the site from spam, abuse, automated traffic and security issues.
  • To maintain basic records of reported issues and fixes.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate StudyDoc, such as Cloudflare and Formspree, or where required by law.

Your choices

You can avoid submitting personal information by not using report/contact forms. You can clear saved quiz progress from the question bank reset option or by clearing your browser's site data for studydoc.co.in.

Legal note

This privacy notice is intended as a clear, practical notice for a small educational website. It should be reviewed by a qualified professional if StudyDoc later adds payments, paid mentorship, newsletters, user accounts, sponsorship tracking, or more advanced analytics. For official Indian data-protection materials, see the MeitY data protection framework.
